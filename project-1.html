Contree

Android

Contree is a unique way to share, split and settle your expenses and pool in money among friends, roommates, college students and colleagues with free bank transfer. It is a powerful personal money manager for all your shared expenses. What makes Contree different as an Expense Management solution: Free bank transfer and single tap to share expense with instant money view and settlement feature. Also, off line functionality and small size ensures that your data pack and phone memory is not taken up unnecessarily.
